Community Meetings: Outreach

As our city grows, outreach is important!.

When coming to make sustainable development decisions, the public is always consulted. The City Of Toronto offers near monthly meetings on these topics, and offers meetings for each specific project proposed. In order to keep up with these meetings, the official website of the city of toronto includes links to any such meetings whether the scope is specific or broad. These meets provide invaluable knowledge about how city planning works, and an inside look at just how one of the largest cities in the world steps forward into their next sustainable phases.

How Can Kids Get Involved In Sustainable Development?

The youth are the future leaders, so why not involve them in building their future

As our city grows, youth voices are increasingly important. For youth over 18, the City Of Toronto has created a Youth Research Team that focuses in on listening to young voices and specializes in designing for the next generation. Their main areas of focus are Schools and Education, Youth Hubs and community centres, Promotion, Engagement and Communication as well as creating ambassadors eager to make their voices heard for change. These opportunities are only set to grow and involve even younger kids with great aptitue and interest for sustainable development and creating sustainable cities and communities.
